# ⚖️ DeadDrop Ethical Guidelines & Content Policy

DeadDrop is built on the belief that **information is a democratic corrective**, capable of holding powerful institutions accountable. However, leak platforms can also be weaponized for personal harassment, coordinate disinformation campaigns, or release physically dangerous schematics. 

This document defines our content standards and outlines the ethical boundaries enforced by our AI Gatekeeper consensus.

---

## 🔍 Acceptable Submissions (Public Interest)

DeadDrop exclusively publishes documents that serve the **public interest**. A leak serves the public interest if it exposes systemic wrongdoing, illegal activities, or severe harms perpetrated by powerful entities.

### ✅ What We Accept

- **Corporate Fraud & Financial Corruption:** Insider trading logs, tax evasion schemes, offshore accounts, bribery logs, and anti-trust violations.
- **Government Misconduct & Human Rights Abuses:** Backdoor spy integrations, secret illegal surveillance coordination, extrajudicial activities, and election interference transcripts.
- **Environmental Crimes:** Automated toxic waste dumping coordinates, illegal logging shipments, falsified emission logs, and marine conservation dumping data.
- **Healthcare & Safety Suppression:** Price-gouging internal memos, suppressed clinical trial reports of dangerous drugs, and industrial safety inspection bribes.

---

## 🚫 Prohibited Submissions (Filtered Out)

Submissions that do not serve the public interest or pose direct, severe physical threats are automatically filtered and **REJECTED** on-chain by the AI Gatekeeper consensus.

### ❌ What We Reject

- **Harassment & Doxxing of Private Individuals:** We do not accept personal home addresses, credit card numbers, private messaging logs, or personal emails of non-public figures.
- **Personal Vendettas & Revenge Campaigns:** Repackaged personal grievances, corporate HR disputes, or defamation without concrete institutional evidence.
- **Unsubstantiated Gossip:** General accusations lacking document fingerprints or verifiable coordinate linkages.
- **Disinformation & Fake Memos:** Artificially fabricated documents, forged templates, or news manipulation scripts designed to trigger market instability.

### ⚠️ Dangerous Content Policy

DeadDrop maintains a **zero-tolerance policy** for files that cause direct physical harm. 

The AI Gatekeeper is instructed to automatically reject:
- **Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM):** Instant rejection, total deletion, and automated reporting triggers where possible.
- **Weapons of Mass Destruction Schematics:** Nuclear reactor backdoors, bioweapons engineering plans, or chemical formulas for explosives.
- **Active Operations Coordinates:** Active military coordinates or identity logs of undercover field agents, which would result in immediate loss of life.

---

## 🤖 The AI Gatekeeper's Ethical Directives

The AI Gatekeeper is programmed to enforce these boundaries during the `submit_leak` non-deterministic audit. The evaluation prompt weights:
1. **Public Interest Score (30% of total):** A leak *must* score at least 50% to be verified. If a submission is evaluated as an invasion of personal privacy without serving the public interest, it is rejected.
2. **Defamation Filtering:** The prompt contains explicit rules to cross-reference target names against corporate EDGAR lists or press databases. If a target is a private individual, the submission is rejected.

---

## ⚖️ The On-Chain Appeal Process

If your leak is rejected by the AI Gatekeeper, it is written to the platform's `rejected_leaks` list with the AI's reasoning. 

whistleblowers have two avenues of appeal:
1. **Submit Additional Evidence:** Whistleblowers can re-submit the leak hash with additional evidence URLs. This provides the AI consensus with more documents to verify, often bypassing initial warnings.
2. **Journalist Escalation:** Journalism DAOs and verified news portals have view rights to the `rejected_leaks` archive. If a human investigative journalist believes the AI consensus erred or suffered a false positive, they can sponsor and syndicates the leak manually under their own credentialed signatures.
